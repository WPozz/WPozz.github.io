My Projects

Finite Element Analysis and Modeling of a Lumbar Vertebra

Starting from a diagnostic image of the L3 lumbar vertebra, obtained via CT, a segmentation was performed using the multi-platform ITK-Snap software. The geometry was subsequently manipulated and modeled using the COMSOL Multiphysics analysis software. A comparison between healthy and pathological case showed that is was indeed possible to model a vertebra and simulate osteoporotic conditions to assess potential fracture risks. Case.
